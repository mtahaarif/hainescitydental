Our experienced team provides a full range of oral surgery procedures in a comfortable, caring environment.

## Teeth Extraction

Safe removal of severely damaged, infected, or problematic teeth, including wisdom teeth extraction.

## Alveolar Plasty

Recontouring of the jawbone to create a smooth, even surface for denture placement or implant procedures.

## Socket Preservation

Bone grafting performed immediately after extraction to preserve the socket and maintain bone volume for future implant placement.

## Bone Graft

Augmentation of jawbone density and volume to support dental implants or restore facial structure after tooth loss.

## Gum Recontouring

Reshaping of gum tissue to improve aesthetics and create a more balanced, attractive smile line.

## TMJ Treatment

Diagnosis and treatment of temporomandibular joint disorders to relieve jaw pain, headaches, and bite problems.

We prioritize your comfort and safety during all surgical procedures, offering sedation options to ensure a relaxed, pain-free experience.
