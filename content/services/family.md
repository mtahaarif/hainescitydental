At Haines City Dental, we are excited to share our passion for oral health with you and your family. Our staff members are committed to providing you with the highest level of service.

## Adult Care

Comprehensive dental services for adults including:
- Preventive care and cleanings
- Restorative treatments
- Cosmetic enhancements
- Periodontal therapy
- Emergency care

## Teen Care

Specialized care for teenagers addressing their unique dental needs:
- Orthodontic evaluations
- Wisdom teeth assessment
- Sports mouthguards
- Cosmetic options for confidence
- Education on proper oral hygiene

We accept and welcome new patients all the time from ages 12 years old to 112 years!

Our goal is to help you be relaxed and comfortable throughout your treatment. We will answer any questions that you may have, go over all of your options and recommend the best course of action for your unique needs.

We know our patients not only want healthy teeth, they want to look good. We will make every effort to meet your expectations and continue to work until you are satisfied. We will be happy to work with you and tailor your treatment at a pace that is convenient for you.
